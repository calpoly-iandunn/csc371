Course Schedule

The course schedule is below. For each day, the due Reading, Lecture content, and assigned Assignments are shown.

You are responsible for having completed all required reading when you show up for class. For example, Chapter 1 is the reading for Wednesday 1/11, so you should have read that chapter by 3:10pm on Wednesday 1/11. There may be a quiz on any assigned reading material!

However, the listed assignments for any given day are the assignments that are assigned that day. They will be due at some later date. Click on each assignment to see additional details.

Textbook

The textbook for this course is Game Design Workshop: A Playcentric Approach to Creating Innovative Games by Tracy Fullerton. I have the 2nd Edition so feel free to get that version. It can be found in a few places:

  • Cal Poly Bookstore (ordered, I will update when I know more)
  • amazon.com (as low as $14 used)
  • chegg.com ($18 for a 90-day eTextbook rental)
